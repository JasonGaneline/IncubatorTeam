Our App Idea

app designed primarily for pregnant women, but could also be used by women curious about pregnancy

purpose: relieve stress, provide information/community, keep them secure during pregnancy

pages/features

SignUp / LogIn w/ User authentication

1. daily mental health check-in (evaluation)
    returns statistics on mood swings / emotions
    track progress over time
    allows them to reflect on how they are feeling each day

2. (Potentially) - AI chatbot trained on pregnancy and birth, mothers can report symptoms and receive advice
    can report abnormalities throughout pregnancy. RAG feature that uses AI with database information to suggest some kind of personalized support

3. in-app emergency feature - notify doctor and emergency services

4. Reddit-style community forum for pregnant mothers where they ask questions, share information, etc.
    post
    reply (notifies you when others reply to you)
    upvote/downvote system
    can follow other profiles and dm
    anonymous posting option (can request to follow/dm)

5. profile
    displays:
        mental health check-in progress
        followers/following
        direct messaging feature
        representation of size of baby based on # of weeks pregnant
            e.g. size of an apple, orange, watermelon, etc.
        estimation of when you will hear heartbeat, find out gender
        tell mother what specifically is happening in her body at any point during pregnancy
            e.g. baby's organs are being developed
